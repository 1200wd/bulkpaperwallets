How to create a software wallet

Install bitcoin wallet software on your phone or PC. Mycelium is a good easy to use wallet for Android and Electrum is a nice wallet for your PC which is safe, easy to use and has a lot of functionality.

When you create a new wallet remember to create a backup, preferably write down your keys and password on a piece of paper. If you lose your phone you can restore your bitcoins with these keys, but if you lose your keys your bitcoins are lost. There is no way to recover them.

If you choose to install Mycelium on your phone , you can easily transfer the Bitcoins from this paper if you go to the main menu and select 'Cold Storage', then select 'QR code' to scan the Private Key on this paper.
