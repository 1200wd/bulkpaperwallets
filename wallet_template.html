Create a wallet

Install a bitcoin wallet on your phone or PC such as Mycelium (or Electrum or any other wallet). When you create a new wallet remember to create a backup, preferably write down your keys and password on a piece of paper. If you loose your phone you can restore your bitcoins with these keys, but if you loose your keys your bitcoins are lost. If you choose to install Mycelium, you can easily transfer the Bitcoins from this paper if you go to the main menu and select 'Cold Storage', then select 'QR code' to scan the Private Key on this paper.
